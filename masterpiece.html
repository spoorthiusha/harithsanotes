Masterpiece

All These Words are Not mine, These Are The Words Written Now Coz I am a Failure now. This Doesn't Mean I am not Successfull. It Means That I love Failures Coz It Makes Me Masterpiece!
April 5th, 2016
Vishruth Harithsa| Life

Is That Marks/Grades, Punctuality and Descipline Says u All About a Person??? If That So Then My Question is What about Patience, Determination, Tempermant And Elegance? If People Stands fr Above 3 Then They Must Stand fr Support To the Below 4. I heard Many Talking About Determination, Temperment....... and Giving Speeches But Nobody Has The Confidence of Telling That i am Giving Importance to them! I Understand Them And Their Situation. Before Talking About Those We Need To Classify Ourselves That Which Suits us The Most.

May be Marks/Grades Are Important to u. If it is then Go for it and Get it then train others who are in ur track to achieve That. Never Drag The People Who are in other Track to urs. Motivate The People Who Follows Not The People Who is Following others or Following Themselves.

Be Great and Be A Dynamic Model to The People. Donot Preamt As a Static Machine! Life is Dynamic Means Why You wish to be a Static Machine???

Simply Achievement is Not A Big thing in your Life! Be like You are The World. Always Do That thing Which Satisfies Your Ideas! Compromise To urself For ur Ideas,. If u Dont have an Idea, follow Other Ideas But remember..... Through Those U Must need To Develop ur Own idea. Finally U need to Get Out With an Idea Which Can Influence numerous Lives. Live Upto ur Breath and Let Others Breathe!

World of Life is So Huge and Filled With Utter Collections of Failures. Bundles of Failures Called Experience and This is The Masterstroke of ur Life 'The Masterpiece'. Being Successfull Fellow don't make u as True Champ But Masterpiece Do. Never Satisfy For Something. Learn Every Second of ur Life.

All These Words are Not mine, These Are The Words Written Now Coz I am a Failure now. This Doesn't Mean I am not Successfull. It Means That I love Failures Coz It Makes Me Masterpiece!
